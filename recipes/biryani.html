Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 lb (450g) bone-in chicken pieces (thighs, drumsticks)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • Cashews and raisins (for garnish)

Steps

  1. In the same pan used for frying onions, add the marinated chicken and cook until it's no longer pink. Remove from heat.
  2. In a large pot, start layering the biryani. Begin with a layer of rice, then add a layer of chicken, some fried onions, chopped cilantro, and mint leaves. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used up.
  3. Drizzle a pinch of saffron strands soaked in warm milk over the top layer of rice for added aroma and color.
  4. over the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for about 20-25 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  5. Once cooked, remove the pot from the heat and let the biryani rest, covered, for another 10-15 minutes. This helps the flavors to fully develop.
  6. Gently fluff the biryani with a fork to mix the layers. Serve the Chicken Biryani hot, garnished with more fried onions, chopped cilantro, and mint leaves. Enjoy your flavorful masterpiece!
