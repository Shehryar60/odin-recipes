Steam Chicken

Description

Indulge in the delightful flavors of a steamed chicken recipe that promises both health and taste in every bite. Steaming is a cooking technique that preserves the natural juiciness of chicken while infusing it with a symphony of aromas and spices. This recipe offers a wholesome alternative to fried or pan-fried chicken, making it a perfect choice for those seeking a lighter yet equally satisfying meal.

Whether you're looking for a healthier option or simply craving a dish that harmonizes taste and nourishment, this steam chicken recipe is your answer. Serve it alongside a refreshing salad or your favorite steamed vegetables to create a well-balanced and satisfying meal that's as delightful to the senses as it is to your well-being.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Pieces: Choose bone-in chicken pieces like thighs or drumsticks for extra flavor and juiciness.
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: A blend of ginger and garlic paste will infuse the chicken with aromatic flavors.
  • Soy Sauce: A splash of soy sauce adds a savory umami taste to the chicken.
  • Herbs (Thyme or Rosemary): Fresh herbs like thyme or rosemary can be added for an extra layer of fragrance and taste.
  • Salt and Pepper: Basic seasonings like salt and pepper enhance the overall flavor of the chicken.
  • Lemon Juice: A squeeze of fresh lemon juice brings a zesty and tangy element to the dish.

Steps

  1. Marinate Chicken: Coat chicken pieces in ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, and lemon juice for flavor.
  2. Add Herbs: Sprinkle fresh thyme or rosemary for aromatic notes.
  3. Season: Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Steam: Place marinated chicken in a steamer and cook until fully done.
  5. Check Doneness: Ensure chicken is cooked through by checking its internal temperature.
  6. Serve: Plate the tender steam chicken and enjoy with your favorite sides!
